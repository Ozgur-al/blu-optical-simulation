<objective>
Wire SolidCylinder and SolidPrism geometry into the multiprocessing tracer path (`_trace_single_source`) and fix the `_run_multiprocess` sb_stats merge to include cylinder/prism stats.

Purpose: Cylinder and prism solid bodies are invisible to rays when `use_multiprocessing=True` because `_trace_single_source` never expands them into face objects and never performs intersection/dispatch for type=4/5 hits. This gap means any scene with cylinders/prisms silently produces wrong results in MP mode.

Output: `_trace_single_source` handles cylinder/prism geometry identically to `_run_single`; `_run_multiprocess` merges cylinder/prism sb_stats.
</objective>
